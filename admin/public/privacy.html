Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

Welcome to the MPNMJEC Bus Tracker application. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile and web applications.

1. Information We Collect

To provide an efficient transportation intelligence system, we collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: We collect real-time location data from driver devices to show bus positions on the map. Student devices do not transmit location unless explicitly allowed for proximity alerts.
  • User Profile Info: For authorized access (Staff/Students/Drivers), we store your name, email address, and role as provided by the college administration.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device (model, OS version) to ensure application stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To display real-time bus locations to students and staff.
  • To calculate estimated time of arrival (ETA) at various stops.
  • To manage transit schedules and fleet operations.
  • To enhance the safety and efficiency of the college transportation system.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption protocols via Supabase. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information or real-time location data with third-party advertisers or external organizations. Access to tracking data is strictly limited to authorized personnel of MPNMJEC.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate information.
  • Discontinue use of the app and request data deletion through the college transport office.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact the MPNMJEC Transport Department or the Information Technology cell.
