# Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to MPNMJEC Driver.

## Overview

MPNMJEC Driver is a transport operations app for authorized MPNMJEC bus drivers. The app helps drivers view assigned trips, manage trip status, and share bus location updates with the MPNMJEC transport tracking system.

## Information The App Uses

The app may use the following information:

- Driver login credentials and driver profile information
- Assigned route, bus, and trip information
- Device location while a driver is using the app for an active transport trip
- Trip status updates such as started, paused, resumed, completed, or cancelled
- GPS telemetry such as latitude, longitude, speed, heading, timestamp, and accuracy

The app may temporarily store GPS points on the device when network connectivity is unavailable. Buffered points are uploaded to the tracking backend when connectivity returns.

## Location Use

Location access is central to this app's purpose. The app uses location data to show the live position of an active college bus trip to transport administrators and student-facing tracking screens.

The app may use foreground and background location permissions so tracking can continue during an active trip when the driver switches apps or the screen is locked. Location tracking should be used only for official MPNMJEC transport operations.

## Information Sharing

The app does not sell user data and does not contain third-party advertising.

Trip and location information is sent to the MPNMJEC tracking backend so authorized transport systems can display live bus tracking, trip progress, and route status.

## Data Security

The app uses HTTPS and secure WebSocket connections to communicate with the tracking backend. Drivers should keep their device and operating system updated for the best security.

## Data Retention And Deletion

Server-side driver, trip, and telemetry data is managed by the MPNMJEC transport tracking system. Temporary offline GPS buffers are stored locally only until they can be uploaded or cleared by the app.

## Children's Privacy

This app is intended for authorized MPNMJEC drivers and transport staff. It is not designed as a child-directed app.
